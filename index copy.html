IEEE Aerospace and Electronic Systems Society CHAPTER

L'AESS est une société professionnelle axée sur les systèmes électroniques intégrés et les technologies connexes. Ses membres bénéficient de nombreux avantages, dont la participation à des comités, des conférences et des prix techniques. Tous reçoivent le magazine Aerospace and Electronic Systems et une réduction sur les transactions, couvrant divers domaines technologiques tels que la commande, la navigation et la robotique.

Mission and Vision

The AESS mission is to create an inclusive professional society, engaging a diverse membership globally, through technical activities. Its vision is to be essential in aerospace and electronic systems, recognized for contributions through conferences, publications, and education.

Provide a responsive and relevant professional society.

Attract, engage, aid, and retain a diverse set of members worldwide.

Accomplish through technical activities in conferences, publications, education, and member services.

Foster inclusivity and engagement through diverse technical initiatives.

IEEE AESS ENIS SBC Previous Testimonials

Hello, my name is Ayoub Abdelkefi, and I serve as the Vice Chair of the AESS chapter in 2022. Throughout my life, I’ve been captivated by Star Wars movies and the theories put forth by scientists. Watching these films has fueled my motivation to explore everything related to space. As the Vice Chair, I’ve taken on a significant mandate this year, working alongside my friends and the dedicated chapter crew. Together, we’ve achieved something remarkable. I’m incredibly grateful for the opportunities this role has provided. Through our chapter, I’ve connected with numerous individuals who are actively contributing to advancements in our country’s space-related endeavors. We’ve also shared a wealth of knowledge with IEEE members, discussing topics ranging from stars to space exploration and airplanes. In conclusion, my experience within this chapter has been truly enriching, and I wholeheartedly encourage others to join us on this exciting journey.

Saul Goodman
